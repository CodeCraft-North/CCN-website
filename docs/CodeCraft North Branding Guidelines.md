# CodeCraft North Branding Guidelines

## 1. Brand Overview

CodeCraft North offers professional, helpful, and growth-focused web services for small businesses. The brand tone is confident, modern, and supportive, with a focus on clarity, usability, and results.

---

## 2. Logo

- **Primary Logo:** Icon on the left, logotype on the right
- **Icon Only:** Use where space is limited (favicons, social avatars, etc.)
- **Clear Space:** Maintain at least the height of the "C" around the logo
- **Minimum Size:**
  - Full logo: 120px width
  - Icon only: 32px width

## 5. Icon Usage

**Style:** Sharp, clean geometry to match the Inter typeface.

- Use in social profiles, footers, and favicons
- Avoid distortions or low contrast placements
- Keep consistent sizing across platforms

---

## 6. Image Guidelines

- Use clean, modern photography with tech-forward themes
- Where possible, include screenshots or UI illustrations that convey clarity and progress
- Icons and illustrations should be minimal, vector-based, and avoid heavy detail
- Ensure images are responsive with proper `srcset` and `sizes` attributes
- Include `width` and `height` attributes to prevent layout shift (CLS)
- Use lazy loading for images below the fold

---

## 7. Voice and Tone

- Professional, helpful, calm
- Avoid jargon; explain complex ideas simply
- Always focused on client growth and long-term results
- Use British English for all copy
- Be confident without being boastful
- Prioritise clarity over cleverness

## 8. Usage Examples

### Primary Button

**Light Mode:**
- Background: `#2563eb` (Primary Blue)
- Text: `#ffffff` (White)
- Hover: Slightly darker blue with shadow lift

**Dark Mode:**
- Background: `#2563eb` (Primary Blue)
- Text: `#ffffff` (White)
- Hover: Slightly darker blue with shadow lift

### Secondary Button

**Light Mode:**
- Background: `#ffffff` (White)
- Text: `#2563eb` (Primary Blue)
- Border: `#cbd5e1` (Slate-300)
- Hover: Background changes to primary blue, text to white

**Dark Mode:**
- Background: `#1e293b` (Slate-800)
- Text: `#f1f5f9` (Light Slate)
- Border: `#475569` (Slate-600)
- Hover: Background changes to primary blue

### Section Layout

- Primary sections: `#ffffff` (light) / `#0f172a` (dark)
- Alternate sections: `#f8fafc` (light) / `#1e293b` (dark)
- Cards: `#f1f5f9` (light) / `#1e293b` (dark)

### Highlights and Accents

- Use `#f97316` (Orange) for:
  - Focus states (accessibility)
  - Special call-to-action accents
  - Important notifications or badges
- Use `#4f46e5` (Indigo) for:
  - Secondary pricing tiers
  - Hover states
  - Supporting brand elements

---

## 9. Favicon and Icons

- **Favicon Sizes:** 16x16px, 32x32px, 64x64px (retina), SVG preferred
- **Social Media Icons:** 400x400px minimum, white or brand blue background
- **Logo Export:** Always export as SVG and PNG (WebP for web use)
- **Apple Touch Icon:** 180x180px for iOS devices

---

## 10. Accessibility

- Maintain WCAG AA compliance (minimum 4.5:1 contrast for body text)
- Use semantic HTML elements (`<nav>`, `<main>`, `<section>`, etc.)
- Ensure all interactive elements have visible focus states
- Include proper ARIA labels where needed
- Ensure keyboard navigation works for all interactive elements
- Test with screen readers
- Never rely on colour alone to convey information
